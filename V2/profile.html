The studio

We treat a restaurant the way a studio treats a design problem.

Twin Tiger Chefs is not only a kitchen and not only a consultancy. It is a studio built on two disciplines that a great restaurant needs in equal measure: the art of what reaches the plate, and the craft of the systems that put it there every day.

We work alongside owners from the first idea to a steady, profitable operation. Sometimes that means shaping a concept and a menu. More often it means building the standards, training, and routines that let good food arrive the same way on every shift.

Art on the plate is what all five senses remember. Craft makes the five-hundredth just as unforgettable.

The artist

Chef Tina

Food begins with feeling.

Tina treats food as a language. Years across refined kitchens have given her an eye for balance, restraint, and the small details that make a dish stay with you. She shapes culinary identity, menus, and the guest experience.

On a project she is the one asking what a restaurant should make people feel, and then making sure every plate answers. She gives a restaurant its voice.

The craftsman

AK Solankky

Behind an effortless restaurant is a room designed to work.

Solankky builds the foundation that lets the food succeed. Drawing on decades in hospitality and kitchen engineering, he designs the flow, the systems, and the operating discipline a restaurant runs on.

He is the one making sure the concept survives a full house on a Saturday night. He gives a restaurant its structure.
