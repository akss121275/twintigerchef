Pre-opening checklist (final 30 days)

The essentials that have to be true before you serve a paying guest.

  • Licences and permits confirmed and displayed
  • Kitchen commissioned and deep-cleaned
  • All recipes costed and signed off
  • Staff trained and rota published
  • Vendor list and order guides live
  • Soft-open date and snag process set
