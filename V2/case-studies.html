Great food, empty tables on a Tuesday

Problem

A well-reviewed kitchen was busy on weekends and quiet midweek, with profit disappearing into waste and inconsistent shifts.

Approach

We standardized recipes and prep, rebuilt the midweek menu around margin, and put a simple daily control routine in place.

Result

Midweek covers steadied, food cost dropped several points, and the owner stopped working every service to hold quality together.

One great restaurant, no way to open a second

Problem

An owner wanted a second location but everything lived in their head, so the concept could not be repeated.

Approach

We documented the operation into a blueprint: recipes, SOPs, roles, and an opening plan a new team could follow.

Result

The second site opened on a clear playbook and matched the original within its first months.
